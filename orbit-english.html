Your Journey

What to Expect

From training to touchdown, here's what your space journey will entail.

STEP 01

Pre-Flight Training

Undergo comprehensive training at our state-of-the-art facility, including gravity simulation, emergency procedures, and spacecraft familiarization.

STEP 02

Launch Day

Prepare for liftoff with our experienced crew, complete final checks, and board your spacecraft for the journey of a lifetime.

STEP 03

Ascent Phase

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Space travelers must be in good physical health, with no serious medical conditions. We conduct a thorough medical examination before acceptance. While no specific fitness level is required, a basic level of physical fitness is recommended. Most healthy individuals between the ages of 18-75 can qualify for our suborbital flights.

Suborbital flight training takes 3-5 days, focusing on basic safety procedures and spacecraft familiarization. Orbital mission training requires 3-4 months of intensive preparation, including zero-gravity simulations, emergency procedures, and spacecraft systems training. Lunar mission training is the most extensive, taking 6-12 months to complete.

Your safety is our top priority. Our spacecraft are equipped with multiple redundant systems, emergency abort capabilities, and advanced life support systems. All flights are preceded by comprehensive safety checks, and our crew undergoes extensive training in emergency procedures. We also maintain a 24-hour mission control center monitoring all flights.

Flight schedules may be adjusted due to weather conditions or technical reasons. In the event of cancellation, we offer flexible rescheduling options or a full refund. We recommend booking well in advance and allowing for some flexibility in your travel plans. All reservations include a 10% refundable deposit, with the balance due 30 days before launch.

We provide luxury accommodations at our training facility for the duration of your training program. All meals are included, prepared by our in-house chefs to meet your dietary requirements. Training facilities include state-of-the-art simulation equipment, fitness centers, and relaxation areas. We also offer transportation to and from the training facility and launch site.
